Detailed Description

The DigitalActuator class models a generic actuator. It changes the output of a digital output to HIGH or LOW. Actuator can have two different state ON or OFF and works in positive (HIGH means ON and LOW means OFF) or negative (HIGH means OFF and LOW means ON) logic.

smrtobj::io::DigitalActuator::DigitalActuator ( )

Default Constructor. It initializes the state of the actuator to switched off and the output pin to 0. The actuator works in positive logic by default (HIGH: turn on, LOW: turn off).
