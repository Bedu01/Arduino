Detailed Description

Class IAQ2000 models the I2C iAQ2000 sensor. The iAQ2000 is designed to sense the level of volatile organic compounds (VOCs) such as smoke, cooking odors, bio-effluence and pollutants. This sensor gives a direct and reliable correlation to CO2 levels: output in ppm (parts-per-million): predicted CO2 concentration based on human induced volatile organic compounds (VOC) detection (in ppm VOC + CO2 equivalents)
